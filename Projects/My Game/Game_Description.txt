🎮 Game Overview

- Title: (TBD)
- Genre: Action, Story-driven, First-Person Shooter (FPS)
- Mode: Primarily Single-player (Multiplayer may be added later)
- Core Objective: Progress through missions to reach your "Final Destination"
- Gameplay Style:
  - Real-time action
  - Mission-based structure
  - Story-driven progression
  - Optional Free-Roam Mode unlocked after completing story mode
  - FPS with optional 2D mode
  - Camera follows the player at all times
  - Task: Navigate a "W" path on the screen, starting at the top left and ending at the right end of the "W"
  - Upon completing the task, a pop-up displays "You WIN!!!" and the game closes in 7 seconds
